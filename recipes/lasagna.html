Lasagna

A classic recipe of one of the most loved foods in the world. The ultimate comfort food = homemade lasagna.

Family and friends gather around the dinner table at any time for this lasagna recipe. A big warm comforting hug within layers of pasta, tomato sauce, white sauce and melted cheese!

Ingredients

  • Pasta Sheet
  • Tomato Sauce/Bolognese
  • White Sauce
  • Ground Beef
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 200 degrees
  2. Put layer of sauce with beef on bottom of tray
  3. Cover pasta sheets evenly over sauce layer
  4. Cover layer of sauce with beef over pasta layer
  5. Cover beef with reasonable amount of cheese
  6. Cover cheese with layer of pasta sheet
  7. Continue this process until desired layers achieved
  8. On top of final layer put beef sauce, white sauce and cheese
  9. Bake in oven until cooked to desirable amount
